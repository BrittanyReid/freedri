About

Hi, I'm Bri! I'm a bi enby and my pronouns are they/them. I love art, cosplay, writing and computer shit. I'm currently studying Computer Science and live in Australia. This website cost me $8 so thats why its here lol.

I love Naruto and Persona, please check out my tumblr and deviantart if you'd like to see my fanart! I mostly draw art for my OTP LeeGaa.

As for my original art, I love to paint watercolour landscapes and portraits, which you can find under 'oc gallery' or on my various social media. I paint both traditionally and digitally, incorperating elements of my traditional technique into my digital art. I love the use of layering coloured details and the contrast with solid off whites, black and the white canvas.

Occasionally I do fantranslations, which you can find on my tumblr blog!
